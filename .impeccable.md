# Yact Design Context

## Users

**Target Audience**: Outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking and mountain climbing

**Context**: Users are adventure seekers who want to:
- Share their outdoor experiences with a community
- Discover new trails and climbing routes
- Connect with like-minded adventurers
- Plan group trips and expeditions

**Emotional Goal**: Feel inspired, energized, and connected to nature and fellow adventurers

## Brand Personality

**Three Words**: 冒险 (Adventurous) • 活力 (Energetic) • 真实 (Authentic)

**Voice**: Inspiring, encouraging, and grounded. Not overly polished—real experiences from real adventurers.

**References**:
- **Patagonia** - Environmental consciousness, outdoor authenticity, purposeful design
- **AllTrails** - Utility-first approach to outdoor experiences
- **Instagram** - Photo-first, clean modern design that puts content front and center

**Anti-References**:
- Generic social platforms with purple gradients and Inter font
- Overly tech-focused designs that feel disconnected from nature
- Corporate or sterile interfaces that lack outdoor soul

## Aesthetic Direction

**Visual Tone**: Minimalist Natural - Clean, modern design that celebrates outdoor photography and authentic experiences

**Core Theme**: "Forest & Sunrise" - Deep forest greens (#2D4A3E) paired with warm sunrise accents (#FFB74D)

**Design Principles**:
1. **Content First**: Let outdoor photography shine—UI should recede, not compete
2. **Natural Rhythm**: Use organic spacing and motion that feels like breathing in fresh mountain air
3. **Earthy Sophistication**: Natural colors with refined execution—rugged but polished
4. **Adventure Energy**: Strategic use of accent colors to create excitement and guide action
5. **Quiet Confidence**: Don't shout—let the experiences speak for themselves

## Color System

- **Primary**: #2D4A3E (Deep Forest Green) - Trust, stability, nature
- **Secondary**: #A9C9A4 (Sage Green) - Calm, growth, harmony
- **Accent**: #FFB74D (Sunrise Orange) - Energy, warmth, adventure
- **Background**: #F5F5F5 (Warm Gray) - Neutral canvas for content
- **Dark Mode**: Deep forest tones that feel like being in the woods at twilight

## Typography

- Avoid: Inter, Arial, system defaults
- Use: Distinctive pairing that feels organic and modern
- Reference: Patagonia's bold editorial headlines + clean body text

## Layout & Spacing

- Generous white space to let photos breathe
- Large border radius (16px) for modern, friendly feel
- Grid-based layouts that feel structured but not rigid

## Accessibility

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Ensure contrast ratios meet standards in both light and dark modes
- Consider reduced motion preferences
- Color-blind friendly palettes
